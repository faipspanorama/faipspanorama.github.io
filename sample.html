From the Editor's Desk


Greetings!!

It is our privilege and honour as Editors in Chief to introduce the first issue of FAIPS Panorama - a magazine put together by the students of FAIPS. Not too long ago,around 4 months back,we were informed our school would be closed and we realized we might not be meeting in person for a few days or weeks.Since then though, it’s become clear that this could be a much longer haul.These unprecedented,unnerving times may go on and on.

However,we were determined.It is said, “When one door closes, another one opens”. Our editorial team found the open door amidst this pandemic which has held the world in a standstill.

Even though we could not meet you ‘physically’, we found means to communicate with you virtually to answer your queries and receive your contributions for this magazine. We are amazed, to say the least, by the immense number of articles, poems and stories we have received.

For this, we would like to thank our respected teachers for giving us such a wonderful opportunity to produce this magazine, for constantly encouraging us to give our best and for guiding us throughout the process of publishing. Moreover, this magazine is not the outcome of the efforts put in by one student alone, but it is the hard work put forward by our editorial team as a whole. We feel blessed to have had the opportunity to work with such talented individuals. To the entire art and graphics team, thank you for putting up with our midnight Zoom calls and last minute demands. To all our managing and assistant editors, thank you for helping us go through the millions of contributions we received and editing them with absolute sincerity. This magazine would not have been what it is without your creativity and ideas. 

We have all put in our best to make this magazine entertaining and congenial to you all. We hope you enjoy it. Happy Reading !

Regards,
