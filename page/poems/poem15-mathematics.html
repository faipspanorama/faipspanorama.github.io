Poem on Mathematics

Mathematics is full of fun with so much to learn

Gains are added ±

While losses are subtracted ±

Degrees are multiplied ×

Percentage is divided ÷

Geometry is full of mystery %

Algebra has a big history αβ

Integers as different as brothers

Lines are parallel --------------

Angles are similar └

Maths is necessary in life

Without it, it is difficult to survive

Husaina Vahed Rangwala, V-B
