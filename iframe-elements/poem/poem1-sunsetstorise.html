MY SUN SETS TO RISE AGAIN

The SUN to the world is the source of life,

It is a beacon of a light, which shines and spreads its grace.

To me, my SUN is my HOPE.

My SUN wakes me up from the deep slumber of darkness,

And leads me to the future.

Days may come, days may go,

Seasons may come, and they may go.

I need not to worry. I just need to close my eyes.

And my SUN is there, in front of me.

Okay, I agree, I cannot see my SUN I can’t understand how it rises or sets, but I’m sure of one thing

My SUN always RISES

And never SETS

I am extremely proud of possessing my SUN

Although it may be present in everyone.

Yet, in my heart of hearts,

I always feel “My SUN is the worlds ENVY and my PRIDE”.

Because my SUN sets to rise again…

K. PRATHEEKA, 5-A
